Our Services

What We Provide

  • Root Canal

    A root canal is a dental treatment used to save a tooth that is badly infected or decayed. The dentist removes the damaged part inside the tooth, cleans it, and fills it to stop the pain and infection. It helps you keep your natural tooth instead of removing it.

  • Alignment Teeth

    Teeth alignment is a treatment to make your teeth straight and properly arranged. If your teeth are crooked or not in the right place, a dentist may use braces or clear aligners to fix them. This helps improve your smile and makes it easier to clean your teeth.

  • Cosmetic Teeth

    Cosmetic teeth treatment is done to make your teeth look better. It includes whitening, fixing broken teeth, or changing the shape of teeth. These treatments help you get a brighter and more beautiful smile.

  • Oral Hygiene

    Oral hygiene means keeping your mouth, teeth, and gums clean and healthy. You should brush your teeth twice a day, floss daily, and visit the dentist regularly. Good oral hygiene helps prevent cavities, bad breath, and gum problems.

  • Live Advisory

    Live advisory means getting real-time help or advice from an expert, like a doctor or dentist, through a phone call, chat, or video. It helps you get answers quickly without visiting the clinic. This is useful for solving small problems or asking questions anytime.

  • Cavity Inspection

    Cavity inspection is the process of checking your teeth for holes or damage caused by decay. The dentist looks for dark spots, pain areas, or tiny holes in your teeth. Early cavity checks help fix the problem before it gets worse.
